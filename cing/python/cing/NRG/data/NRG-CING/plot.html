Plots

Many CING validation criteria are also in the RDB and over 50 plots such as the ones below are available as Overall plots and as Trending plots (deposition date on x-axis). For many criteria a selection of entries was made. The entries were selected to have at least: 3.5 kDa molecular weight, 10 models, and one protein chain. The raw image files are located here for Overall plots and Trending plots.

Examples

Figure 1 shows a histogram with the distribution of the backbone normality check (called BBCCHK in What If) per residue over the same selection of entries as detailed above. You can click on the image to inspect the the high-resolution version. The dashed red curve is a bell-curve fit. Clearly this histogram does not follow a normal distribution.
Figure 2 shows a histogram of the NOE completeness up to 4 Angstrom at the entry level as calculated by Wattos.
Figure 3 shows a slight increase over the years in the new quality check values as calculated by What If.
