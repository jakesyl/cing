NRG-CING presents a complete validation report for all 9,000+ wwPDB NMR entries including remediated experimental data such as chemical shifts from BMRB and restraints from NRG . These CING reports are compiled from internal analyses and those by CCPN, DSSP, PROCHECK-NMR/Aqua, ShiftX, Talos+, Vasco, Wattos, and WHAT_CHECK.

The below query facility will allow you to perform database-wide searches on the underlying files. Please consider doing one of the Tutorials with screencasts. The image links to the specific CING validation report whereas the PDB and BMRB identifiers link to the original data.
