RECOORD CING Validation Reports

RECOORD CING Validation Reports BETA

These reports are based on the coordinate data of RECOORD after the final stages of water refinement. The experimental data is the same as NRG-CING. The image itself links to the entry's CING validation report. Please reference the relevant papers.

Additional local resources:
  • A CSV dump files of the Postgresql RDB is present here TODO. The database contains information at the levels of entry, chain, residue, & atom. Most recently the distance restraints have been added.
  • You may have to dig into the data; e.g. to find the summaries for any one entry go here where br are the 2nd & 3rd characters of the entry 1brv.
  • The log files can be found like here sorted by date & time of when the validation was done.
  • The preparation of the data was carried out automagically by scripts using functionality in Wim Vranken's FormatConverter and CING.
